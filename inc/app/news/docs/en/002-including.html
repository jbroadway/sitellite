Including News App into your web site

To include News App into your web site, include the box "news/index" via one of the following methods:

  • Include this tag in your templates: <xt:box name="news/index" />
  • Provide a link to this URL: http://www.yourWebSite.com/index/news-app
  • Create a page that is an alias to the path: /index/news-app
